MindM8 – Data Deletion Policy

This page explains how users of MindM8 can request the deletion of their personal data in compliance with privacy regulations.

🧠 App Name & Developer

App: MindM8 – Minds Need M8
Developer: Oryvo AI

📝 How to Request Deletion

Users can delete their data directly through the MindM8 app:

  1. Open the MindM8 app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Clear All Chats
  4. Confirm the action when prompted

🔐 What Data is Deleted

Upon deletion request, the following data is permanently removed from our Firebase database:

  • All journal entries and GPT chat messages

⏳ Retention Period

Once a deletion request is submitted, data is removed instantly. No data is retained after deletion.
